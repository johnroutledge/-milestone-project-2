Pop Bang Boing

Score as many points as you can in 30 seconds.

The game starts from the top circle in a clockwise direction.

The action word determines the next circle:

  • Pop = click adjacent circle in the same direction
  • Bang = click adjacent circle in the opposite direction (also reverses the direction)
  • Boing = click next-but-one circle in the same direction
